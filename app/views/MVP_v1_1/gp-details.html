Book an appointment online

You can book an appointment online if:

  • you’re a registered patient with this surgery
  • you have a username and password for this surgery’s online services website

If you don’t have a username and password, ask reception for a form to register for online services.

What to do when the surgery is closed

When the surgery is closed, you can still book an appointment online.

If you think you need to see someone more urgently, call 111 for advice on where to get help.
