Who can order a repeat prescription

You can order a repeat prescription if a GP has:

  • prescribed you medication for a health problem before
  • said that you need to take the medication regularly
  • put the medication on your repeat prescription list

Your GP will tell you how often you can get a repeat prescription. You won’t be able to get a repeat prescription more often than that.

Contact reception

You can call reception to ask for medication that is on your repeat prescription list.

Get a repeat prescription online

You can use this service to ask the surgery to:

  • prepare your repeat prescription
  • leave the prescription on reception for you to pick up
  • nominate a pharmacy that you would like to pick up the prescription from
  • send a repeat prescription to a pharmacy you’ve nominated before
