Test results you can ask for

You can ask for the results of a:

  • blood test
  • x-ray
  • ultrasound scan

Before you ask for results

For blood tests, wait at least a week before asking for results.

For x-rays and ultrasound scans, wait 2 weeks.

Don’t ask receptionists for test results. Receptionists aren’t qualified to comment on results.

Don’t book a GP appointment to get your results.

Get test results on the phone

Call reception and ask for the test results service.

The service is open from 9am to 12.30pm Monday to Friday.

01234 5678901 open now

Get test results online

The online test results service doesn’t tell you the exact results of your test. It tells you whether the GP would like to see you to talk about your results.

If your test showed nothing unusual, the GP will not ask to see you.

If something unusual has shown up in your test, the GP will ask to see you. This doesn’t mean you’re going to get bad news, so try not to worry.
